Research

My research focus lies at the intersection of machine learning, computer games, AI planning and plan recognition, and more recently, deep learning. I am particularly interested in studying how machine learning can be used to construct behavioral/player models from training data, and how these models can subsequently be utilized by intelligent agents for reasoning about/over actions in a domain.

Research Areas:

  • Machine Learning/Deep Learning
  • AI Plan and Goal Recognition
  • AI Planning
  • Computer Games
